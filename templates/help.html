The homepage explained

 The What’s popular homepage lets you do the following:

  • In the City Searches section, you can explore what is popular in each city of United Kingdom by entering a city name in the Search bar
  • In the Example section, the will be examples teaching you how to use the website

The pie chart explained

 This result is presented by a pie chart, each part of which represents the proportion of a category that is interested by people of the city you are searching for. Only top 5 topics would be shown on the pie chart. There are 25 topics in total, including Animals & Pets, Antiques & Collectibles, Architecture, Art & Photography, Auto & Cycles, Business & Finance, Children, Comics & Humor, Computers & Electronics, Cooking, Food & Beverage, Education, Ethnic, Fashion & Style, Health & Fitness, History, Literary, Medical, Music, News & Politics, Psychology, Religion, Science & Nature, Sports & Recreation, TV & Movie, Weather. You can click on a topic to get more information, like the most relevant tweets.
